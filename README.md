# Online Food Delivery Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes online food delivery operations using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to identify business insights related to customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, revenue generation, and operational trends.

## Key Insights

- Hyderabad generated the highest revenue among all cities.
- Indian cuisine contributed the highest revenue share.
- Adult customers placed the majority of orders.
- Card payments generated the highest revenue.
- Peak hours accounted for approximately one-third of total orders.
- Late delivery was the leading cancellation reason.
